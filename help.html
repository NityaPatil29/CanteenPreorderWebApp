Help

Need assistance? We're here to help! Below are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) to guide you through our platform:

How do I pre-order my food?

Simply log in to your account, select your preferred canteen, choose your meals, and proceed to checkout. Your food will be ready by the time you arrive.

What if I need to cancel or modify my order?

If you've made a mistake in your order, please contact us immediately. We can help you modify or cancel your order as long as it's before the preparation starts.

Can I pay online?

Yes, we support online payment methods to ensure a seamless and contactless experience.
